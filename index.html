Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 01, 2026

Your data never leaves your device

Overview

SpeedSpend is a personal finance tracking application designed to be fast, simple, and private. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how SpeedSpend handles your data.

Data Collection

SpeedSpend does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data you enter into the app — including transactions, projects, and categories — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

No Account Required

SpeedSpend does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or payment details.

Third Party Services

SpeedSpend optionally uses the Frankfurter API (frankfurter.dev) to fetch currency exchange rates. This request contains no personal data — only a currency code is sent to retrieve publicly available exchange rate information. You can view Frankfurter's privacy information at frankfurter.dev.

Data Security

Since all your data remains on your device, you are in full control of it at all times. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all data stored by SpeedSpend.

Children's Privacy

SpeedSpend does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.
